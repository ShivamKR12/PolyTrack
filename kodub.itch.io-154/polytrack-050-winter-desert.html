PolyTrack 0.5.0 - Winter & Desert

PolyTrack » Devlog
38 days ago by Kodub
Share this post:
Share on BlueskyShare on TwitterShare on Facebook

PolyTrack 0.5.0 is a major update introducing many new features, including Winter and Desert environments, the ability to race or watch up to 10 ghosts simultaneously, checkpoint respawning and more.

The editor has also been updated allowing track parts to be overlapped, rotated around any axis and placed more precisely using a smaller grid.
